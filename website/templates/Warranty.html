Warranty Information

At Agegracefully, we stand behind the quality of our products and offer a limited 1-year warranty on all of our walking sticks.
If you experience any issues with your walking stick, please contact us and we will work with you to resolve the issue.

Why the warranty?

  • Quality Assurance
  • To create a safer environment for the elderly.
  • Allow us to serve you better

What is covered by the warranty?

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Breakage due to normal use
  • Material defects

What is not covered by the warranty?

  • Damage caused by misuse or abuse
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Modifications or alterations made to the product

If you have any questions about the warranty or need to file a claim, please contact us.
